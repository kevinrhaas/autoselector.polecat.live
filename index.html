Three ways in

Finding a car should be the fun part

No 40-field filter walls. Pick the finder that matches your mood — they all end at the same place: a shortlist you actually love.

Match Maker

Answer friendly questions — budget, people, buttons-vs-screens, snow — and watch a live counter whittle 326 cars down to a handful. "Not sure" is always an answer.

Lifestyles

First car? Growing family? Comfort & confidence? Weekend adventurer? Pick the driver you are and every vehicle gets ranked for that life — with reasons.

Budget Explorer

Slide the money and watch the market respond in real time. Add seats, efficiency and powertrain toggles, then hand your result to the full filter grid.

The details that matter

Filter by full-size spare, physical climate and audio controls, light or dark interiors, ground clearance, towing — the living-with-it stuff other sites bury.

Compare & Garage

Heart your contenders, add private notes, and line up four side-by-side — best numbers glow green. Undo anything with ⌘Z.

Honest data

Every record carries a confidence flag and provenance notes. Pre-release estimates say so, right on the page — no silent guesses.

The catalog

Every 2026 vehicle. Actually every one.

From the $20k Chevy Trax to the $3.1M Ferrari F80 — compiled from manufacturer announcements and price guides, and re-verified continuously by an automated improvement loop.

326

models on sale in the US for 2026 — cars, SUVs, trucks, vans, EVs, exotics.

47

brands with parent manufacturers, logos, and good-resolution photos.

20+

attributes per vehicle: trims & MSRPs, powertrains, MPG/range, dimensions, cargo, seats, spare tire, physical controls, interior colors, options.

SQL-ready

The whole catalog maps onto a documented SQLite/Postgres schema — export schema + seed from Settings.

How it works

Three steps to your car

1

Pick a finder

Match Maker for guided questions, Lifestyles for instant persona shortlists, Budget Explorer for the money-first view.

2

Heart your contenders

Favorites land in your Garage with private notes. Everything is undoable, and it all stays on your device.

3

Compare and decide

Four side-by-side, best values highlighted. Copy a link to any car to share it.

New here? The app opens with a 60-second welcome tour, has full built-in documentation, and a Simple mode for calmer screens. It improves itself every hour — check what's new and what's next.
